Dr. Michael A. Soderstrand, Ph.D.

Professor, Retired

Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering

University of California, Davis

For more than six decades, Prof. Soderstrand's professional life has been intertwined with the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of California, Davis. During that time he has served as professor, educator, researcher, and mentor, teaching thousands of students and contributing to the growth of the department.
