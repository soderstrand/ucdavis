Publications

Research Summary

During more than four decades as a researcher and professor, Dr. Michael A. Soderstrand authored and co-authored an extensive body of scholarly work spanning active analog filters, digital signal processing, residue number arithmetic, adaptive filtering, FPGA and VLSI implementations, wireless communications, engineering education, and computer engineering. His publications include peer-reviewed journal articles, conference papers, books, book chapters, and edited IEEE Press volumes.

Highlights

  • More than 200 peer-reviewed journal articles and conference papers
  • Author or editor of several books and book chapters
  • Research spanning 1969 through the present
  • Publications in IEEE Transactions, Proceedings of the IEEE, IEEE Press, and many international conferences
